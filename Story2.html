Project Highlights

  • Title: Bank of Baroda Snapdeal Credit Card – Co-Branded Launch Campaign
  • Platform: YouTube + Meta

2. Objective

To launch the exclusive BoB-Snapdeal co-branded credit card by positioning it as the ultimate online shopping companion — delivering instant rewards, cashback, vouchers, and deals that make every purchase feel like a win, while driving high-volume card applications through Snapdeal’s massive eCommerce audience.

3. How It Was Created

  • Designed bold, high-energy visuals with a dominant red-pink palette to align with both BoB and Snapdeal branding, using playful illustrations and relatable shopping scenarios (clothes, groceries, deals).
  • Structured into three complementary storyboard flows: a direct CTA closer (“So what are you waiting for?”), a detailed benefits explainer (vouchers, 5–10% cashback, EMI, 100+ deals), and a fun rhetorical question series (“What’s Shopping for you?”) to spark engagement and FOMO.
  • Incorporated real-time offers (₹500 voucher, 10 reward points per ₹100, auto-credited cashback) with clear, benefit-focused copy and branded card visuals for instant recognition.
