BOBCARD

Scaling Credit Card Acquisition for a Leading Asian NBFC

1. Project Highlights

  • -68% Acquisition Cost: CPL: ₹1,450 → ₹465 | Analytics-driven funnel optimization
  • +35% Monthly Card Approvals : 800 avg. cards | 12-month sustained growth
  • +42% Brand Awareness Lift : Pre: 38% → Post: 54% | Targeted digital branding
  • 850K+ Mentions HandledReal-time engagement across social & review platforms

2. The Challenge

The client, a leading Asian non-banking financial company with core expertise in credit cards and merchant acquiring, focusing on rapid digital expansion in India, faced significant MARCOM & PPC challenges:

  • Needed to scale digital acquisition processes and enhance the digital user journey to meet aggressive growth targets
  • Required management of high user volumes and reduction of response times to compete in a crowded market

3. Our Solution

We implemented a comprehensive MARCOM & PPC strategy to dominate digital acquisition and drive high-intent credit card applications:

  • Unified Data IntegrationLeveraged DataStream Suite to consolidate Google Ads, Meta, Programmatic, and CRM data across Tier 1 & 2 cities (Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore, Pune, Hyderabad) for consistent performance tracking.
  • Campaign Segmentation Segmented campaigns by audience income (₹8L+), lifecycle stage (pre-approved vs. open market), and channel, with tailored creatives (e.g., “0% EMI on Amazon with [Card]”) to improve relevance and intent.
  • Real-Time Performance TrackingUtilized Visual Forge dashboards to monitor CPL, CPA, application drop-offs, and approval rates in real time, enabling rapid creative and bid adjustments.
  • Budget OptimizationReallocated budgets to high-converting micro-segments (e.g., 25–35yo tech professionals) using A/B testing and predictive LTV modeling to maximize ROI.

4. Results

  • -68% Reduction in Acquisition CostCRO + funnel analytics streamlined the application journey (from 7 steps → 3), dropping CPL from ₹1,450 → ₹465 while scaling volume.
  • +35% Rise in Monthly Card ApprovalsTargeted branding + retargeting delivered 800 average monthly approvals, sustained over 12 months via lookalike audiences and dynamic creatives.
  • 850K+ Mentions Handled AI-powered social listening + 24/7 response team managed brand queries, complaints, and applications across Twitter, Instagram, and Google Reviews, converting 18% into leads.
