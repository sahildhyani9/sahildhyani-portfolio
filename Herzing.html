2. The Challenge

A renowned U.S.-based education institution with strong programs in nursing, healthcare, business, and technology, known for its flexible online and campus offerings, faced several constraints:

  • Attract U.S.-based transfer students within a compressed 5-week campaign window
  • No redesign allowed for the transfer landing page
  • Strict compliance restrictions on messaging and content
  • Deliver quality leads quickly despite institutional limitations

3. Our Solution

We built a highly agile performance marketing strategy:

  • Hyper-focused regional targeting on high-potential U.S. markets.
  • Tailored messaging highlighting MBA advancement, credit-transfer ease, and immigration support.
  • Positioned Herzing as the most seamless transfer option with compelling value props.
  • Mobile-first campaigns to maximize lead generation despite landing page constraints.

4. Results

  • 181 new leads generated in just 5 weeks.
  • Final cost per lead dropped to $29 in the closing phase.
  • 5x reduction in CPA compared to generic campaigns
